About me

Hi I'm Sabrina Roestenburg, an aspiring front-end developer and homeshool mom. Welcome to Gluten-free tested and approved.

About a year ago, I learned that one of my daughters had to start a dairy and gluten-free diet. (and to make it even easier coconut, palm oil and cacao are a no-no too!) As a family that eats lots of bread, pasta and pancakes it was quite an adjustment. For pasta I quickly found a decent store bought substitute made out of brown rice, but for bread, pancakes, cupcakes,cookies etc, I had come up with my own game plan. There are thousands of recipes available online but I soon found out that not all of them are as good as they say they are. After much trial and error we have gathered a notebook full of recipes that we like, and like to share.
